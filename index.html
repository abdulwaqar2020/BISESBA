Our Responsibilities

Infrastructure Management: Our IT department maintains and supports hardware and software infrastructure, including servers, networks, and databases. This ensures reliable access to digital resources for staff and students.


Data Management and Security: Managing student and staff data, ensuring its security, and protecting against breaches. This includes implementing cybersecurity measures and compliance with data protection regulations.


Training and Professional Development: Offering training sessions and resources to help educators and staff effectively use technology in their roles.
