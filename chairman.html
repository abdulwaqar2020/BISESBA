From 08-04-2019 To-Date

Chairman Message

I am pleased to announce that the Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education, Shaheed Benazirabad, has revised and relaunched its website, now more informative and interactive than ever before. As one of the oldest educational boards in the region, we take pride in our long-standing tradition of excellence in education. Over the years, we have produced a significant number of secondary and higher secondary education graduates who have gone on to excel in various fields, achieving remarkable success and making meaningful contributions to society. However, it is with a sense of urgency that I address a concern: the quality of our examinations has seen some decline over time, which, in turn, has impacted the overall standard of education in the province. This is a matter of serious consideration, and we are committed to addressing these challenges head-on. Our aim is to not only maintain but also enhance the quality of education, ensuring that our students are well-prepared to meet the demands of an ever-evolving world. As we move forward, we invite all stakeholders—students, teachers, parents, and community members—to engage with us through this new platform. Together, we can work towards revitalizing our educational standards and upholding the legacy of excellence that BISE Shaheed Benazirabad stands for.
