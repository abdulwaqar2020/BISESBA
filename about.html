Welcome To Board of Intermediate & Secondary Education Shaheed Benazirabad

BOARD HISTORY

The Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education, Shaheed Benazirabad is one of the newest boards which was established in 2019.The creation of BISE Shaheed Benazirabad aimed to decentralize the examination process in Sindh, making it more accessible and manageable for students and educational institutions in the Nawabshah region. The board works under the guidelines of the Sindh Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education and the Education Department of Sindh, Pakistan. The name change from Nawabshah to Shaheed Benazirabad was in honor of the late Benazir Bhutto, the former Prime Minister of Pakistan, reflecting her contributions and legacy in the region and country. The board continues to play a crucial role in the educational landscape of the region, striving to improve the quality of education and the integrity of the examination process. Currently, the Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education, SBA conducts its secondary and intermediate examination in three(03) districts. These include; Nawabshah, Sanghar & Noshero Feroz. The Board has produced a large number of alumni which included educationists, researchers, politicians, heads of State, parliamentarians, judges, lawyers, engineers, civil servants, scientists, human right activists, philanthropists, social reformers, poets, writers, scholars, economists, historians, archeologists, architects, industrialists, agriculturalists and others.
